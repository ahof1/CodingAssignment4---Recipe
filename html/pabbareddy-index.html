The Perfect Summer-y Parfait

Recipe By: John Canell of Preppy Kitchen


Ingredients

  • Choice of yogurt (plain, greek, vanilla etc.)
  • Granola
  • Mango
  • Peaches
  • Blueberries
  • Honey

Rate This Recipe

How difficult is this recipe? Easy Intermediate Hard

=

Instructions

  1. Take all your fruits and chop them into bite size/about half inch pieces and set aside
  2. Scoop 1/3 cup of your choice of yogurt into the bottom of your jar
  3. Add granola and fruits to your liking
  4. Add a drizzle of honey to your liking atop the fruits and granola
  5. Apply another 1/3 cup of yogurt ontop of your fruit layer
  6. Continue to repeat the layering process until you've reached your desired amount
  7. After your final yogurt layer garnish with more fruit, granola and honey
  8. Finally ENJOY!
