The Ultimate Hawaiian Pizza Recipe

Adapted recipe

  • Recipe adapted from Cooking For My Soul:
  • https://cookingformysoul.com/hawaiian-pizza/

    • Ingredients

  • Homemade pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup deli ham
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks, drained
  • Dried oregano to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

    • Directions

    • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    • Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella on top of the sauce.
    • Evenly distribute ham and pineapple chunks.
    • Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
    • Remove from oven, let cool slightly, slice, and serve.
